What's Ranger?

Ranger is an Eclipse plugin for finding relevant tests on your workspace.

How does it work?

Similar to the existing call hierarchy plugin. You choose a method, and search for tests. The plugin returns all tests, across all projects in your current workspace, whose execution can potentially go through the chosen method. It's that simple!

Is it free?

Of course, duh!
